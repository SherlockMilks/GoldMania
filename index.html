How to Play

Players take turns. Click on a field to move there.

If the distance is within your movement range, your unit moves and your turn ends.

Watch the timer! Your turn also ends automatically when time runs out (turn time can be adjusted).

Capture mines to generate gold each turn. You can capture enemy mines if your damage is high enough.

You can upgrade the health of your captured mines by moving onto them.

Return to your castle to upgrade damage, movement range, and gold multiplier.

To win, capture the enemy castle.

Keyboard shurtcuts: S - Save game, L - Load game, I - Hide/show info

Good luck and have fun!
